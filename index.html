Our Story

Mansa Suit & Sarees

Mansa Suit & Sarees is your trusted neighbourhood destination for ethnic fashion in the heart of Jaipur's Barkat Nagar, Tonk Phatak area. We offer a wide and carefully curated range of women's clothing — from everyday salwar suits and kurtis to elegant sarees and dazzling party wear lehengas — with sarees starting at just ₹400. Whether you're dressing for a regular day or a special celebration, we have something beautiful for every occasion and every budget.

Located at Shop No. 152-153, opposite Indian Public School, Sachivalaya Colony, Adarsh Bazar, our store is open seven days a week from 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM for your convenience. With a strong reputation for fair pricing, good quality, and a welcoming shopping experience, Mansa Suit & Sarees has become a favourite among local families. Come visit us and discover why our customers keep coming back for more.
